# Dynamic Programming

Dynamic programming (DP) is an approach to solving problems through 'clever bruteforce'. It is a method of solving a problem by breaking it down into smaller subproblems, and then solving those subproblems recursively.

DP is no easy topic to master and thus it would do you well to stay committed to learning DP and not give up in the face of shortcomings.
